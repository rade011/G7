Our Offer

As a host, you will receive a total peace of mind. You would hand us the keys to your property and we would take it from there. There are no long contracts or lock-in periods.

Hosting Support

We offer three packages. Two packages include full service and are completely hands-off and we also have a lite (basic) package for those who only need partial support.

Golden Package

Pass on your keys and forget about the property. Watch the money flow into your account. This package includes:
-A visit to your property to discuss with you if there are any simple/quick changes to the property that can improve the appeal of the listing. This alone, and followed by a professional photo shoot, could potentially massively improve your listing appeal.

Pass on your keys and forget about the property. Watch the money flow into your account. This package includes:
-Managing of airbnb profile, potential queries, calendar etc. We manage your profile, photos,
interactions and reviews. We integrate calendars and collate bookings.
-All the interaction with the guests both via the airbnb, phone, skype, WhatsApp. We offer a local Greek phone number as well.
-Interaction in person. We are present for the check-in, check-out and any emergency. The check-in includes a property and area orientation tour. The check-out includes collecting a feedback from the guest and any other help needed with the onward journey.
-Greeting the guests at the check in and check out. This is a very important aspect of the overall guest experience. We know that and we are very good at it.

Platinum Package

a little extra that helps you stand out in the crowd. In addition to the services in the gold package, the platinum package includes:

-our linen and towels. We provide a high quality linen and towels, that we use in our hotels, to give your property that professional feel. The sheets and towels are Cocomat and 100% cotton.
-Our toiletry pack. We provide a high quality toiletry pack of daily essentials from our local partner
-Welcome basket of organic greek products to give guests a taste of the best Greece has to offer
-payment processing
Price: 25% from your booking

Lite Package

Get yourself a professional virtual receptionist and an administrator. You will have a dedicated person, who knows you and your property dealing with all types of online and telephone interactions and administration. We will communicate and coordinate with you or your dedicated person at the property. This package gives you a peace of mind that you will never miss a booking and your guests will always get the quick, professional and friendly reply they expect. We all know how important first impressions are. Let us help you with that. The lite package includes:

-Managing of airbnb profile, potential queries, calendar etc. 
-All the interaction with the guests both via the airbnb, phone, skype, WhatsApp. We offer a local Greek phone number as well.
-With your help and input, helping the guests find themselves around the property and the city
-Managing guest reviews and listing score
-With your help and input, managing the airbnb damage insurance claims
-professional photographs
-Generating invoices
- organising transfers, taxi, tours, ferry tickets, luggage storage, experiences for your guests etc.*
-payment processing*
*same conditions apply as in the golden package.
Price: 8% from the booking value
